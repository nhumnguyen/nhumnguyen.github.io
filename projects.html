QUICKPOLL


Worked as a frontend developer for an Android app which helps user get immediate responses from people in the nearby area, by creating or responding to any daily poll. This app targets users who share the same interests, such as students in a class who have questions and need immediate answers from other students.
Implemented using Java, Android Studio, Adobe Illustrator.
